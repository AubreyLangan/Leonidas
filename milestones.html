Milestones

Leo's milestones will be listed here as he grows. Check out some of his recent photos in the photo gallery!

Go to Photo Gallery

Birth

Leo was born on January 6th, 2024. The best birthday gift that I could ever give my husband.

First Night Home

Leo spent his first night home on January 7th, 2024. He hated his bassinet at first. It was a rough first night home.

First Doctor's Appointment

Leo went to the doctor for the very first time on January 10th, 2024. He was 4 days old and HATED having to be stripped naked to be weighed and measured. He had lost almost a pound since leaving the hospital and his bilirubin levels were a little high.

First Hospital Stay

On January 11th, 2024, Leo had his first overnight stay at the Mary Bridge Children's Hospital. We went to the doctor's earlier that day to have his bilirubin levels checked again and found out that they were still too high. We spent the night at Mary Bridge where Leo spent most of our stay under the bilirubin lights. He wasn't a fan at first but eventually calmed down. What a trooper! He was 5 days old.

First Time Meeting His Grandparents

My parents flew all the way from Guam on February 3rd, 2024 to meet Leo for the very first time. He was 4 weeks old.

Leo Turns One Month Old

Leo got to spend his one month birthday with his grandparents and Aunt Alli.

Leo's First Valentine's Day

Leo's first major holiday that he got to celebrate was Valentine's Day! At just a little over a month old, Leo wore a super cute outfit with fake suspenders and a bowtie for breakfast with G and Grandpa before they left to go back to Guam. Then, the three of us went bowling to celebrate my husband and I's wedding anniversary. Leo slept through our trip to the bowling alley.

Leo Turns Two Months Old

Leo got to spend his two month birthday snuggled up with Mommy and Daddy.

Leo's First St Patrick's Day

Leo celebrated St Patrick's Day wearing a super cute green outfit. He also got to meet his godparents for the very first time.

Leo's First Easter

Leo celebrated his first Easter dressed up in a super cute bunny outfit and getting his first Easter basket. His first Easter basket consisted of a new rattle, some boats for bath time, bubbles, new clothes and some candy for mommy and daddy.

Leo Turns Three Months Old

Leo spent his three month birthday at home with Mommy and Daddy.

Leo's First Earth Day

Leo celebrated his first Earth Day by making a cute foot painting of the Earth with Mommy.

Leo Turns Four Months Old

Leo spent four month birthday at home with Mommy and Daddy.

Leo's First Professional Photoshoot

The Wednesday before Mother's Day, Leo had his first professional photoshoot with Mommy. The pictures turned out so stinking cute!

Leo's First Mother's Day

Leo spent his first Mother's Day hanging out with Daddy so Mommy could go get her hair done. Once she was home again, they got to have lots of cuddles.

Leo's First Ferry Ride

Over Memorial Day weekend, Leo got to take his first ferry ride! He slept for about half of it and then he woke up and ate for a little bit. The motion of the boat on the Sound seemed to help keep him calm.
